Who We Are

Empowering through Education, Healthcare, and Skill Development

Established in 2004, Sai Educational Society (SES) brings together a team of experts across various fields, all dedicated to making a difference. Registered under the Andhra Pradesh Societies Registration Act-35 of 2001 (Reg. No. 282/2004), our organization stands as a beacon of accountability and transparency.

Our Vision

We aspire to cultivate a society that can rise and empower itself through enhanced education, skill development, and a healthier lifestyle.

Our Mission

To empower our society with exemplary education and healthcare, and to foster the capability to lead fulfilling, independent lives without discrimination.

Education

We believe education is the foundation of development and prosperity. Our primary initiative, Sri Sathya Sai Vidya Vihar, focuses on providing high-quality education to children from underprivileged sections of the community. By eliminating financial barriers, we offer free educational resources, scholarships, and comprehensive support to foster an environment where learning thrives. Our innovative educational strategies are designed to prepare young minds for a future where they can be adaptive, thoughtful, and impactful.

Education

Health Care

Good health underpins societal well-being and productivity. Our Primary Health Centre delivers essential healthcare services at no cost, encompassing medical consultations, screenings, and treatments. Our dedicated healthcare team is relentless in their pursuit of wellness and preventive care, ensuring accessible and quality medical attention for all.

Health Care

Skill Development Centre

In today’s world, relevant skills are the keys to personal and professional success. SES’s Skill Development Centre provides free vocational training, workshops, and mentorship, enhancing job readiness and entrepreneurial spirit. By empowering individuals with necessary skills, we are building a more inclusive and prosperous society.
