Skill Development Centre

In 2016, we established the Skill Development Centre with a focused objective: to equip local youth and women with new skills, enhancing their earning potential and improving their quality of life. This initiative reflects our commitment to community empowerment through vocational training and education.

Program Highlights:

Tailoring and Embroidery Course:
Duration: Three-month intensive training
Participants: Local women
Outcome: Participants gain marketable skills that enable them to earn a sustainable income and enhance their living standards.
Impact: Approximately 70 women have successfully completed the course and are now benefiting from the skills they acquired.

Future Endeavors:

Expansion of Course Offerings:

Plans are underway to introduce basic computer skills training for local unemployed youth, aiming to broaden their employment opportunities and integrate them more fully into the evolving digital economy.

Our Skill Development Centre serves as a catalyst for positive change, empowering individuals with the skills needed to thrive in a competitive job market. Through these programs, we are not only fostering personal growth but also contributing to the socio-economic development of our community.

Education

We believe education is the foundation of development and prosperity. Our primary initiative, Sri Sathya Sai Vidya Vihar, focuses on providing high-quality education to children from underprivileged sections of the community. By eliminating financial barriers, we offer free educational resources, scholarships, and comprehensive support to foster an environment where learning thrives. Our innovative educational strategies are designed to prepare young minds for a future where they can be adaptive, thoughtful, and impactful.

Health Care

Good health underpins societal well-being and productivity. Our Primary Health Centre delivers essential healthcare services at no cost, encompassing medical consultations, screenings, and treatments. Our dedicated healthcare team is relentless in their pursuit of wellness and preventive care, ensuring accessible and quality medical attention for all.
