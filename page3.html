SRI SATHYA SAI VIDYA VIHAR

Established in 2006, our school is dedicated to providing free education to all deserving students regardless of their religion, creed, or social status. Occupying a spacious 2-acre campus, our facilities include airy classrooms and extensive playgrounds designed to enhance the learning experience.

Originally serving 40 students, our institution now educates 450 children from kindergarten through 10th grade, all from underprivileged communities in the surrounding areas. We are committed to not only delivering quality education but also fostering the comprehensive development of our students, ensuring they evolve into well-rounded individuals. Thanks to our exceptional staff and facilities, our school consistently achieves outstanding results in State Board Examinations.

Our core mission is to provide education imbued with high moral values, echoing the philosophy that "the end of education is character." Our faculty consists of 20 highly qualified and experienced teachers covering education from L.K.G. to X class. To date, we have maintained a 100% pass rate in the SSC Public Examinations across five batches, with our sixth batch currently preparing for their assessments.

Our curriculum is enriched with the teaching of moral values, Vedam, Kuchipudi dance, karate, yoga, music, and art & craft alongside academic studies. This holistic approach to education has earned our school the honor of being designated as a LIGHTHOUSE SCHOOL, receiving technical and academic support from the Sri Sathya Sai Vidya Vahini project—a vision of Bhagawan Sri Sathya Sai Baba. This support has enabled us to upgrade all our classrooms to digital learning environments.

The core values of our school are as follows:
Providing free education to the socially and economically disadvantaged in our region.
Encouraging comprehensive personal development to foster well-rounded personalities.
Cultivating a spirit of unselfish service to society, contributing to societal upliftment.
Guiding young minds towards righteousness through education reinforced by faith.
Developing self-confidence anchored in a belief in the divine.
Instilling respect for traditional values, and humility towards parents and elders.
Enhancing communication skills and fostering healthy social relationships among students.

Education

We believe education is the foundation of development and prosperity. Our primary initiative, Sri Sathya Sai Vidya Vihar, focuses on providing high-quality education to children from underprivileged sections of the community. By eliminating financial barriers, we offer free educational resources, scholarships, and comprehensive support to foster an environment where learning thrives. Our innovative educational strategies are designed to prepare young minds for a future where they can be adaptive, thoughtful, and impactful.

Health Care

Good health underpins societal well-being and productivity. Our Primary Health Centre delivers essential healthcare services at no cost, encompassing medical consultations, screenings, and treatments. Our dedicated healthcare team is relentless in their pursuit of wellness and preventive care, ensuring accessible and quality medical attention for all.
