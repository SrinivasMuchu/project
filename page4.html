Primary Health Centre

At Sai Educational Services, we uphold the belief that a healthy population is pivotal to fostering a healthier society. To this end, we launched our Primary Health Centre in November 2019 within the Sai Educational Society premises. Initially closed due to the COVID-19 pandemic, the centre successfully reopened on November 23, 2021, and has since been an essential healthcare resource for the local community.

Services Offered:
  • Medical Consultations, Laboratory Tests, and Medications: Provided free of charge to approximately 700 to 800 visitors each month.
  • Comprehensive Care: Treatment for conditions such as typhoid, malaria, respiratory infections, common colds, fevers of unknown origin, injuries from accidents, allergies, diabetes, hypertension, and various infections.
Diagnostic Facilities:
  • Hemoglobin percentage (HB%)
  • VDRL (syphilis testing)
  • Widal test for typhoid
  • Serum creatinine levels
  • Random blood sugar levels
  • Malaria parasites detection
  • Serum bilirubin levels
  • Urine analysis
Team and Infrastructure:

Qualified Medical Team: Includes an experienced doctor holding an MBBS degree, a certified nurse, and a trained lab technician.

Support Staff: Additional personnel to assist in medical and administrative duties.

Facilities: The Health Centre is housed in permanent buildings owned by Sai Educational Society, located in a spacious, hygienically maintained compound.

Operating Hours: Open 4 to 5 hours daily, equipped with all necessary medical testing equipment.

Education

We believe education is the foundation of development and prosperity. Our primary initiative, Sri Sathya Sai Vidya Vihar, focuses on providing high-quality education to children from underprivileged sections of the community. By eliminating financial barriers, we offer free educational resources, scholarships, and comprehensive support to foster an environment where learning thrives. Our innovative educational strategies are designed to prepare young minds for a future where they can be adaptive, thoughtful, and impactful.
